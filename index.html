EXCEL CLEANING AND ANALYSIS
OF A BIKE STORE

This data analysis project centers around examining and visualizing data from a Bike store using Microsoft Excel, with a specific focus on leveraging Pivot Tables and charts. The primary objectives include determining the total revenue generated by the store, understanding customer demographics, and identifying key performance indicators. The analysis spans various dimensions, providing insights into customer distribution across states, gender demographics, popular products, top-performing sales representatives, and prominent brands. Additionally, the data can be further drilled down to explore details at the city level within states and analyze trends across different years of sales.
